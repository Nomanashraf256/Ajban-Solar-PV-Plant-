🚨 FULL EMERGENCY ACTION PLAN | Immediate Steps & External Coordination
STEP 1 · WITNESS & P.A.S.S.

🔔 First Response & Alert

  • Alert Emergency Manager & Site Security (0509 637 247) – give location, nature.
  • Use P.A.S.S. method if small fire & trained.
  • Isolate hazard, mark area, protect personnel.
  • Call Site Ambulance: 0526 550 063 for medical emergencies.
  • Announce via walkie-talkie “Emergency Code” to all teams.
STEP 2 · ACTIVATE ESCALATION

📡 Internal Alert & Evacuation

  • Emergency Manager determines Minor / Level 1 / Level 2 (see chart).
  • If Level 1+ → Contact Police (999) and Fire (997) immediately.
  • For poisoning/chemical exposure: Poison help 800424.
  • Order evacuation to assembly points. Wardens conduct headcount.
  • For Level 2: Corporate Crisis Management Team (CMT) activated.
STEP 3 · EXTERNAL RESOURCES

📞 Coordinated Emergency Services

  • Liaison officer meets fire/ambulance at main gate (site map ready).
  • Share incident briefing with Civil Defense (997) and Police (999).
  • Site Ambulance paramedics + external EMS if multiple casualties.
  • Use emergency contact sheet displayed in control room, offices, warehouse.
  • Ensure communication with nearest hospitals & poison control if required.
STEP 4 · POST-INCIDENT

📋 Debrief & Continuous Improvement

  • Debrief within 48h; involve external responders if Level 1/2.
  • Complete incident report & Root Cause Analysis for major events.
  • Update Leg Chart & evacuation maps quarterly.
  • Review drill performance & response times (graphs benchmark).
  • Re-train P.A.S.S. & emergency communication protocols.
👥 Key Roles & Emergency Communication Matrix

🛡️ Emergency Manager (O&M HSSE)

  • Declares escalation level; coordinates with Site Security (0509 637 247).
  • Directs external calls: Fire 997, Police 999, Ambulance 0526 550 063.
  • Activates CMT for Level 2 and ensures headcount reports.
  • Approves incident report & leads debrief.

📻 Radio & Alert Group

  • Walkie-talkie emergency group broadcasts evacuation & updates.
  • Secondary contact via cellphones using site directory.
  • Notification to all personnel: “Emergency: [type] – proceed to Assembly Point”.
  • Maintain contact with external agencies until incident under control.

📍 Assembly Point Wardens

  • Direct evacuees to safe zones; conduct roll call.
  • Immediately report missing persons to Emergency Manager.
  • Provide first aid until site ambulance arrives.
  • Ensure evacuation routes remain unobstructed.
