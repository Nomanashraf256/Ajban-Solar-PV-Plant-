🚨 FULL EMERGENCY ACTION PLAN | Immediate & Follow-up Actions
STEP 1 · WITNESS & P.A.S.S.

🔔 Perform First Actions

  • Alert Emergency Manager & Site Security (0509 637 247) → state nature, location, injuries.
  • Assess situation, identify dangers → move clear of hazard.
  • If safe & trained → use P.A.S.S. extinguisher method for small fires.
  • Mark area to prevent secondary accidents.
  • Notify all affected personnel using alarm or voice.
  • Use Emergency Contact Sheet (numbers above: Ambulance, Security, Police, Fire).
STEP 2 · INTERNAL ALERT & ESCALATION

📡 Activate Evacuation & Escalate

  • Emergency Manager + Lead Engineer assess severity (Minor / Level 1 / Level 2).
  • Announce via walkie-talkie emergency group → incident details & evacuation order if needed.
  • If Level 1 or 2 → immediately call Police 999, Fire 997, Site Ambulance 0526 550 063.
  • For poisoning/chemical: Poison Help 800424.
  • All personnel move to designated assembly points → headcount reported.
  • Level 2 triggers Corporate Crisis Management Team (CMT).
STEP 3 · EXTERNAL COORDINATION

📞 Coordinate with Emergency Services

  • Liaison officer meets Fire (997), Police (999), Ambulance at main gate.
  • Provide site layout, incident briefing, and access routes.
  • Site Security (0509 637 247) controls access & traffic.
  • Emergency Manager shares real-time updates with external responders.
  • Ensure all external numbers are used according to escalation level.
STEP 4 · POST-INCIDENT & IMPROVEMENT

📋 Debrief & Reporting

  • Debrief meeting within 48 hours after emergency under control.
  • Capture Lessons Learned and update emergency plan quarterly.
  • Incident Report prepared; for Level 2 → formal Root Cause Analysis (RCA).
  • Update escalation chart & evacuation maps if required.
  • Retrain teams on P.A.S.S. & emergency contact usage.
👥 Key Roles & Communication Protocol

🛡️ Emergency Manager (O&M HSSE)

  • Overall command & determines escalation level.
  • Directs calls to external authorities: Police 999, Fire 997, Ambulance 0526 550 063.
  • Activates CMT for Level 2, ensures headcount & incident reporting.
  • Coordinates debrief & updates emergency plan.

📻 Emergency Group Communication

  • Walkie-talkie network connects all team members instantly.
  • Emergency Manager broadcasts: “Emergency Code, location, assembly point”.
  • Secondary contact via mobile phones & site directory.
  • All external calls use authorized numbers (displayed at top).

📍 Assembly Point Wardens

  • Conduct roll call at assembly points.
  • Report missing personnel immediately to Emergency Manager.
  • Provide first aid and coordinate with site ambulance.
  • Ensure evacuation routes remain clear.
